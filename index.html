Dr. Peter H. Löwe

This is the github.io page of Dr. Peter H. Löwe, the geoscientist (neither the folk singer, boating instructor or other person by this name).

About

Peter holds a PhD in Geography (Dr. rer. nat. - summa cum laude) and has a strong background in Geoinformatics, Remote Sensing, Open Science, A.I. and Digital Research Infrastructures.

Beyond his research, he believes in the great outdoors, travel, gardening, and homemade BBQ.
