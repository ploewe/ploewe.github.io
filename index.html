About

Peter holds a PhD in Geography (Dr. rer. nat.) and has a strong background in Geoinformatics, Remote Sensing, Open Science, A.I. and Digital Research Infrastructures.

Beyond his research, he believes in the great outdoors, travel, gardening, and homemade BBQ.
