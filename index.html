Meet Dr. Peter Loewe!

This is the personal website of Dr. Peter H. Loewe, the geoscientist (neither the folk singer, boating instructor or other person by this name).

Peter holds a PhD in Geography (Dr. rer. nat. - summa cum laude) and has a background in Geoinformatics, Remote Sensing, Open Science, A.I. and Digital Research Infrastructures. He believes in the great outdoors, travel, gardening and homemade BBQ.

  • Studied at the University of Würzburg and the University of Texas at Austin.
  • Work experience at Leibniz and Helmholtz research institutes, the satellite remote sensing industry and a UNESCO Geopark.
  • Founder of the startup FUKENGRUEVEN, supported by the Bavarian FLÜGGE programme.
  • Lecturer at the University of Potsdam, Humboldt University Berlin and Hochschule Neubrandenburg.
  • Evangelist for the international Open Geospatial Community.
  • Elected charter member of the international OSGeo Foundation and also Vice President of Open GeoScience.
  • Open Source GRASS GIS project steering commitee (2016 - 2021).
  • Member of the Research Data Alliance (RDA), RDA Deutschland e.V., Wikipedia and FOSSGIS e.V.
  • Member of the European Geoscience Union (EGU) and the American Geophysical Union (AGU).
  • Elected convener for the EGU programme group “Data, Software and Computing Infrastructures across Earth and Space Sciences” (ESSI2) and invited speaker for the Great Debate 2022 on Open Science.
  • NSF Geospatial Software Institute conceptualization project.
  • Eastern Partnership on E-Infrastructure (EaPEC by GÉANT) in Armenia, Moldova and Belarus.
  • Editor for the Open Source GIS chapter of the Springer Handbook of Geographic Information (2nd. Ed.).
